Beehive Data

High quality data about charitable funding

Beehive Data is part of a family of tools and technology that helps charitable organisations and their funders make the most of their resources when working together. Its purpose is to improve open data about charitable funding, as well as providing an interface for other tools to access and use the information it enriches.
